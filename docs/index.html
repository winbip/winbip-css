Margin

  • margin all: .ma*[-lg/-sm]
  • margin horizontally (left & right): .mh*[-lg/-sm]
  • margin vertically (top & bottom): .mv*[-lg/-sm]
  • margin bottom: .mb*[-lg/-sm]
  • margin top: .mt*[-lg/-sm]
  • margin left: .ml*[-lg/-sm]
  • margin right: .mr*[-lg/-sm]
* = -auto, 0 (0 rem), 025 (0.25rem), 050 (0.50rem), 100 (1.00rem), 150 (1.50rem), 300 (3.00 rem)

For example,

  • .ma150 creates 1.50rem margin on all sides on all devices.
  • .ma100-lg creates 1rem margin on all sides on large devices.
  • .ma075-sm creates 0.75rem margin on all sides on small devices.
